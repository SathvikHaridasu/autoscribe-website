  • Recommended for most users

MacOS Security Bypass

  1. After downloading, find AutoScribe.dmg in your Downloads folder.
  2. Double-click to mount the disk image, then drag AutoScribe to Applications.
  3. When trying to open the app, you'll see a security warning.
  4. Instead of clicking the app icon, Control-click (or right-click) on the app and select "Open".
  5. In the dialog that appears, click "Open" again.
  6. Alternatively, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down and click "Open Anyway" next to AutoScribe.
